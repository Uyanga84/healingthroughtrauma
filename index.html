Welcome to Bayandalai Counseling

I’m Dr. Uyanga Bayandalai, a trauma-informed therapist rooted in Internal Family Systems (IFS), EMDR, and somatic approaches. I specialize in helping women—especially those carrying complex trauma, cultural displacement, or burnout—reconnect with their inner clarity, compassion, and leadership.

Healing through trauma isn’t about erasing your past. It’s about reclaiming your story, honoring your resilience, and coming home to yourself.

How I Work

I offer virtual therapy for adults in Minnesota, using a gentle, client-centered approach that integrates evidence-based modalities. My work draws from:

  • Internal Family Systems (IFS) – helping you explore and heal your inner world with compassion
  • EMDR Therapy – supporting you in safely processing traumatic memories and shifting patterns
  • Somatic & Mindfulness Practices – helping you reconnect with your body and inner wisdom

All parts of you are welcome in this space.
